Alfredo Recipe

Alfredo pasta is rich and creamy, with a velvety sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese. It has a luxurious, buttery flavor, complemented by the nutty sharpness of the cheese. The sauce coats the pasta, creating a comforting, indulgent dish.

Alfredo pasta is a classic Italian dish known for its rich and indulgent flavor. The sauce, made from a simple yet luxurious blend of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, creates a velvety texture that clings to the pasta, offering a comforting mouthfeel. The buttery richness is perfectly balanced by the nutty, slightly sharp taste of the Parmesan, making each bite a decadent experience. Whether served with fettuccine or other pasta varieties, Alfredo pasta is a satisfying dish that embodies the essence of comfort food with its creamy, savory goodness.

Ingredients

  • Butter - 1/2 cup (1 stick)
  • Heavy cream - 1 cup
  • Parmesan Cheese (freshly grated) - 1 cup
  • Garlic - 2 cloves (to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pasta (fettuccine or your preferred type) - 1 pound
  • Parsley (chopped, for garnish) - optional
